How to Use SentReader AI (Chat with Files and More)

01

Sign Up for an Account

  • Visit the official website at myreader.ai.
  • Click "Try for free" to create an account. You can sign up with your email or other quick methods.
  • This gives you access to upload and process files right away. Free trials are available, with paid plans for more features like unlimited uploads.

02

Upload Your Files

  • Select a single document, a collection, or your entire library from the sidebar.
  • Start typing questions in the chat interface, like "Summarize chapter 3" or "What are the key insights on AI ethics?"
  • The AI provides instant answers with in-line citations linking back to exact pages or sections.
  • You can chat across multiple books at once for comparative insights (e.g., "Compare views on leadership in Book A vs. Book B").

03

Chat with Your Documents

  • Once logged in, go to your library or the upload section.
  • Drag and drop or select files to upload: Supports PDFs, EPUBs, Kindle books (AZW – remember to download and remove DRM first), Word docs (.doc), PowerPoint (.ppt), web article links, or even YouTube videos.
  • You can upload up to 20,000 pages total. Files are stored securely in the cloud, kept private, and you can delete them anytime.
  • Tip: Organize uploads into collections (e.g., "Philosophy Books" or "Research Papers") for easier access later.
